About

Aura is a music app that provides you with music based on your current mood! All you have to do when you visit our app is choose your mood, and Aura does the rest. When a user chooses a mood, Aura will provide a playlist with songs to match your mood and intensify your state of mind! Whether you are happy, sad, motivated, or in a party mood, Aura is here to provide music to enhance that mood!

Genres

Aura provides you with music for any mood- good or bad. The genres are matched up by mood, so choosing by genre is not an option. Since music is ultimately fueled by a mood or feeling, this is a more in tune way to capture what you want to listen to at that moment. Aura captures your Aura and plays genres and songs based on that feeling.

Options

Aura is made with simplicity in mind. There is only one option you have- and that is to choose which mood you are currently feeling and let Aura do the rest. Whatever mood you decide on will determine your experience with the app. Enjoy the simplicity and comfort of Aura.
